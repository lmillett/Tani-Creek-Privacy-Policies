Privacy Policy for Prairie Sentinels

Last Updated: April 16, 2026

Prairie Sentinels does not collect, share, or store any personal data. All app functionality operates entirely on your device. No user data is transmitted, tracked, or linked to your identity.

Thank you for choosing Prairie Sentinels! This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Prairie Sentinels does not collect, store, or share any personal information from its users. We believe in protecting your privacy, and as a result, we have designed our app to operate without the need for any personal data or user accounts.

2. Location Data

Prairie Sentinels may request access to your device's location services to provide features based on your current position. This information is processed locally on your device and is not transmitted, stored, or shared externally. You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

3. Analytics and Cookies

Prairie Sentinels does not use any analytics tools, cookies, or tracking technologies. Your app usage and preferences remain entirely private to your device.

4. Third-Party Services

Prairie Sentinels may retrieve publicly available data from government or open-data sources to provide app functionality. These requests do not include any personal identifiers or user data. We do not integrate with advertising networks or other third-party services that collect user data.

5. Children's Privacy

Prairie Sentinels is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. If you believe that we may have inadvertently received such information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. Any updates will be posted here with a revised "Last Updated" date. If significant changes are made, we may display a notice within the app to inform users.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or Prairie Sentinels' privacy practices, please contact us at tani.creek.software@icloud.com.

By using Prairie Sentinels, you agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.

Thank you for using Prairie Sentinels!
